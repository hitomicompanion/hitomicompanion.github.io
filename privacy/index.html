Hitomi Companion Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Hitomi Companion Android app.

1. Data We Process

  • Authentication data: account identifier, sign-in provider, and basic profile fields such as username or email when provided by the login provider.
  • Chat data: messages you send to Hitomi and the responses returned inside the app.
  • Diagnostics: limited app status and error data needed to keep the companion working and debug failures.

2. Android Permissions

  • Microphone is used only when you enable speech input.
  • Overlay permission is used to display the floating Hitomi companion on top of other apps.
  • Termux command access is optional and only used if you explicitly enable local shell tools.

3. Third-Party Services

The app relies on these services to operate:

  • Supabase authentication and backend functions
  • xAI AI responses delivered through backend relay functions
  • Google Play app distribution and closed testing access

4. How Data Is Used

  • To sign you in and maintain your session
  • To generate Hitomi responses and companion features
  • To operate optional Android features such as voice input, overlays, and local Termux shell tools when enabled
  • To monitor reliability and troubleshoot app issues

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with the service providers required to run the app features listed above.

6. Data Retention

Account, session, and chat-related records may be retained as needed for product operation, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

7. Your Choices

  • You can sign out of the app at any time.
  • You can revoke microphone and overlay permissions in Android system settings.
  • You can simply avoid enabling Termux if you do not want local shell tool access.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@agent1c.ai.
